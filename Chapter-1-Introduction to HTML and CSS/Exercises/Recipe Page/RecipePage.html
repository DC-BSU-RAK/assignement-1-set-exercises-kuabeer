Recipe For Pizza

Make perfect pizza at home with this pizza recipe! This is perfect for family and kids, and it can be fun and interactive.! The pizza toppings can be customized to everyone's preferences.

Ingredients list

For Pizza Dough

  • 1 cup warm water(110 degrees F)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 2 ½ cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Toppings

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Pepperoni , thinly sliced
  • Bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • Onions, thinly sliced
  • Sliced black olives
  • Mushrooms, sliced

Methods

  1. Dissolve the sugar, yeast, and salt in the lukewarm water (and olive oil, if you're using it).

  2. Add the flour and Pizza Dough Flavor, starting with 5 1/2 cups (660g) flour and adding more as necessary to make a soft, smooth dough.

  3. Knead by hand on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until the dough is smooth and slightly sticky. add more if its too wet.

  4. Lightly oil a large bowl, place the dough in it, and turn to coat with oil. Cover with plastic wrap. For a quick rise, let it sit in a warm place (75°F-85°F) for 1.5 hours.

  5. Gently deflate the dough, and divide it into four pieces, for medium-crust pizza; or three pieces, for thicker crusts. Use a rolling pin to roll each piece, on a lightly greased surface, into a circle to fit a 12" pizza pan. Let the dough rest several times to relax it

  6. Place the rounds on pizza pans; on baking sheets; or, if you have a pizza stone in your oven, on parchment.

  7. Preheat your oven to 450°F and preaping your toppings.

  8. Brush each crust with a bit of olive oil; spread tomato sauce lightly over the surface. Add toppings on pizza. and finish with cheese.

  9. Bake the pizzas for 15 to 20 minutes, until they're golden brown, the toppings are hot and bubbly, and the cheese is melted. Remove the pizzas from the oven.

  10. Immediately transfer pizzas to a cooling rack, so their bottoms don't get soggy for 10 miuntes.

  11. After 10 minutes cut the pizza
