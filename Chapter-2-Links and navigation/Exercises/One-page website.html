Overview

The Rookie is an American police drama television series that premiered on ABC in 2018. The show follows John Nolan, a forty-year-old man who becomes the oldest rookie at the Los Angeles Police Department after a life-changing bank robbery inspires him to pursue a career in law enforcement. As he navigates the challenges of being a new officer later in life, he must prove himself to his younger colleagues while handling dangerous cases and personal struggles. The series blends action, crime-solving, and character-driven drama with occasional humor, starring Nathan Fillion in the lead role.
